How Traffic Flows

Default (Auto mode)

sing-box urltest probes all 4 servers every 60s. Picks fastest (tolerance 50ms). Usually NL or Russia→DE.

iPhone → Relay 185.218.0.43:443 → DE 162.19.242.30:2096 → Internet

Manual server selection (e.g. Germany)

Selector default changed to "Germany". VPN disconnects, reconnects with new config.

iPhone → DE 162.19.242.30:2096 → Internet (direct, no relay)

DNS resolution

Always goes through Auto urltest (fastest server), independent of Proxy selector.

dns-remote: DoH 1.1.1.1 (detour: Auto)
dns-direct: DoH 8.8.8.8 (detour: direct)
dns-fake: fakeip 198.18.0.0/15
