UX Flows

Сценарии оптимизированы под минимальное число действий. Основная цель: подключить пользователя за 1-2 касания и не перегружать навигацией.

Flow 01: First Launch

Target: первый защищенный запуск за 60 секунд
1. Onboarding screen: value + trial + Continue with Apple.
2. iOS VPN permission prompt.
3. Home (disconnected) with dominant Connect CTA.
KPI: onboarding completion > 85%.

Flow 02: Connect / Disconnect

Target: быстрый контроль состояния туннеля
1. Tap connect on Home.
2. Connecting state with cancel option.
3. Connected state: session timer, active route, subscription pill.
4. Tap orb again for disconnect.
KPI: time-to-connect p95 < 4.5 sec (product metric).

Flow 03: Change Route (No extra tab)

Target: сохранить минимальный IA
1. From Home tap Route card.
2. Open Route Picker sheet with 5-8 options.
3. Select route and apply immediately.
KPI: route change success > 95%.

Flow 04: Renewal

Target: вернуть защиту при истекшей подписке
1. Expired banner on Home and Account.
2. Open Paywall from CTA.
3. Purchase or restore; return to connected flow.
KPI: renewal start rate from expired state > 35%.

Flow 05: Error Recovery

Target: восстановить соединение без ухода в support
1. No internet banner + clear explanation.
2. Primary action Retry, secondary Diagnostics.
3. Copy report and send to support if retry failed.
KPI: self-recovery within 2 attempts > 70%.
